About Uncle Sam Nutrition

Canadian Quality
Global Standards

Uncle Sam Nutrition is a Canadian-registered animal nutrition brand committed to providing premium livestock feed solutions that promote healthier animals, higher productivity, and sustainable farming practices.

Our scientifically formulated feed products are developed using carefully selected ingredients and advanced nutritional principles to support optimal animal health, improved milk production, and better feed efficiency.

Through our strategic partnership with Sam Feeds and manufacturing collaboration with Pashupati Industries in India, we ensure consistent quality, reliable production, and efficient distribution to serve modern dairy and livestock farmers across the country.

  • Canadian-registered brand with global quality standards
  • Manufactured in India by Pashupati Industries
  • Scientifically formulated for optimal animal health
  • Supports higher milk production & feed efficiency
  • Empowering farmers with high-performance nutrition
